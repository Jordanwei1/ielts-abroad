# 雅思听力场景词汇:租房场景

> 发布时间: 2026-03-12  
> 来源: 我的每日听写训练

---

## 📖 场景介绍

租房场景是雅思听力Section 1最常见的场景之一,通常涉及:
- 房屋类型
- 租金和押金
- 房屋设施
- 地理位置
- 租赁条件

---

## 📝 核心词汇 (50个)

### 房屋类型

1. **flat** /flæt/ n. 公寓
   - 例句: I'm looking for a two-bedroom flat.
   - 替换词: apartment

2. **studio** /ˈstjuːdiəʊ/ n. 单间公寓
   - 例句: A studio apartment is perfect for one person.
   - 替换词: bedsit

3. **detached house** 独栋别墅
   - 例句: They live in a detached house with a garden.

4. **semi-detached** 半独立式住宅
   - 例句: Semi-detached houses share one wall.

5. **terraced house** 联排别墅
   - 例句: Terraced houses are common in the UK.

### 租金相关

6. **rent** /rent/ n./v. 租金;租赁
   - 例句: The rent is £800 per month.
   - 替换词: rental fee

7. **deposit** /dɪˈpɒzɪt/ n. 押金
   - 例句: You need to pay a deposit of one month's rent.
   - 替换词: security deposit

8. **landlord** /ˈlændlɔːd/ n. 房东
   - 例句: The landlord requires references.
   - 替换词: property owner

9. **tenant** /ˈtenənt/ n. 租客
   - 例句: The tenant is responsible for minor repairs.
   - 替换词: renter

10. **lease** /liːs/ n. 租约
    - 例句: Sign a one-year lease.
    - 替换词: rental agreement

### 房屋设施

11. **furnished** /ˈfɜːnɪʃt/ adj. 配家具的
    - 例句: The flat is fully furnished.
    - 反义词: unfurnished

12. **heating** /ˈhiːtɪŋ/ n. 暖气
    - 例句: Central heating is included.
    - 相关词: air conditioning

13. **utilities** /juːˈtɪlətiz/ n. 水电费
    - 例句: Utilities are not included in the rent.
    - 包括: electricity, water, gas

14. **appliances** /əˈplaɪənsɪz/ n. 家电
    - 例句: The kitchen has all appliances.
    - 例如: fridge, oven, washing machine

15. **balcony** /ˈbælkəni/ n. 阳台
    - 例句: The flat has a small balcony.

### 地理位置

16. **suburb** /ˈsʌbɜːb/ n. 郊区
    - 例句: The house is in a quiet suburb.
    - 反义词: city center

17. **proximity** /prɒkˈsɪməti/ n. 邻近
    - 例句: The proximity to the station is convenient.
    - 短语: in close proximity to

18. **accessible** /əkˈsesəbl/ adj. 可到达的
    - 例句: The flat is easily accessible by bus.
    - 名词: accessibility

19. **neighborhood** /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ n. 社区
    - 例句: It's a safe neighborhood.
    - 英式拼写: neighbourhood

20. **commute** /kəˈmjuːt/ n./v. 通勤
    - 例句: The commute to work takes 30 minutes.

### 租赁条件

21. **available** /əˈveɪləbl/ adj. 可用的
    - 例句: The flat is available from next month.
    - 名词: availability

22. **contract** /ˈkɒntrækt/ n. 合同
    - 例句: Read the contract carefully.
    - 动词: sign a contract

23. **notice** /ˈnəʊtɪs/ n. 通知期
    - 例句: You need to give one month's notice.
    - 短语: notice period

24. **references** /ˈrefrənsɪz/ n. 推荐信
    - 例句: The landlord requires two references.

25. **inspection** /ɪnˈspekʃn/ n. 检查
    - 例句: There will be a final inspection.
    - 动词: inspect

## 💻 听写练习

### 练习1: 填空题

Listen and fill in the blanks:

1. The flat is ________ (furnished/unfurnished).
2. The ________ (rent) is £850 per month.
3. You need to pay a ________ (deposit) of £850.
4. The ________ (landlord) lives on the ground floor.
5. ________ (Utilities) are included in the rent.

### 练习2: 场景对话

```
A: Hello, I'm calling about the flat for rent.
B: Yes, it's still available. It's a two-bedroom flat.
A: How much is the rent?
B: It's £900 per month, plus a deposit of £900.
A: Is it furnished?
B: Yes, it's fully furnished with all appliances.
A: When is it available?
B: From the 1st of next month.
```
